What Next?

If you find that a career choice is reoccuring or catches your attention, write it down. After you've written them all down, it is now time to do research. Here are some questions you should ask:

How much do they earn?

Understanding the earning potential of a job is vital as it directly impacts financial stability and long-term career satisfaction. Knowing the salary associated with a particular occupation enables you to plan your budgets, cover expenses, and save for the future, ensuring they can maintain your desired lifestyle.

How to get into the field?

Understanding how to enter a specific job field is crucial as it facilitates informed career decisions and strategic planning for professional growth. Knowing the pathways into a particular field allows you to identify the necessary qualifications, skills, and experiences required for success. This knowledge enables aspiring you to tailor your education, training, and networking efforts accordingly, increasing your chances of securing employment in your desired field

How is the job like?

Understanding what a job is like is essential as it helps you make informed career decisions and ensures a good fit between your skills, interests, and the demands of the job. Knowing the nature of the job provides insights into daily responsibilities, work environment, and potential challenges, allowing individuals to assess whetheryou would enjoy and excel in the role.

After you've considered all these and your career in mind has ticked every box. Now it is time to make a roadmap.
